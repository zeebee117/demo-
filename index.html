About IDR

Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital risk, cyber risk, and AI governance. We prepare professionals to assess, model, and control technology risk across modern digital ecosystems.

Working alongside university partners, including collaborations with UK institutions, IDR connects academic rigor to real-world industry projects so graduates can deliver practical impact from day one.
